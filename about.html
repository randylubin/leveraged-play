About

Leveraged Play is founded and run by Randy Lubin, a game designer and entrepreneur. Our organization draws on a rich network of professional game designers who collaborate to make compelling games that help players explore the future and understand the present.

Randy has been designing games for over 10 years, for both consumer, corporate, and non-profit audiences, and has collaborated with world class game designers. In 2021 he received a special award from Next Generation Foresight Practitioners in the Innovative Methods category. You can view his consumer games at his studio Diegetic Games.

He earned his MBA from Stanford and has also been on the leadership team of multiple technology companies in a COO or business generalist role. He also advises and angel invests in tech startups.

Past clients include: World Economic Forum, UNICEF, Mozilla, and Hewlett Foundation.
